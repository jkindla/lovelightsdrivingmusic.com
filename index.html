About



Love Lights Driving are a Los Angeles-based group comprised of Nathan Reyes (vocals/ guitar), Josh Kindla (guitar), Ernie Montaño (bass) and Diego Briones (drums). Nathan, Diego and Ernie grew up in Southern California and met each other in high school. Josh, who met the rest of the band during a musicians conference in LA, is from San Antonio, Texas.

The group calls their music "Pocket Pop”. Their style fuses elements of rock, alternative, R&B, and pop music with disco, funk, indie, Afro-Latin beats, and the synthesizer and arpeggiator/sequencer driven sound of modern electronica.
